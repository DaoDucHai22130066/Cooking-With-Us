Traditional Vietnamese Pho

By Chef Minh Jan 20, 2024 5.0 (24 reviews)
Prep: 30 mins
Cook: 3 hrs
Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

Instructions

1

Prepare the Broth

Clean the beef bones thoroughly. Boil them for 10 minutes to remove impurities, then drain and wash again. Place bones in a large pot with 3 liters of water.

2

Char the Aromatics

Roast the onion and ginger over an open flame until charred. Add them to the pot along with the spice bag (star anise, cinnamon, cloves).

3

Simmer

Simmer the broth on low heat for at least 3 hours. Skim off the foam occasionally to keep the broth clear. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste.

4

Assemble and Serve

Blanch the noodles. Place them in a bowl. Top with sliced beef. Pour the boiling broth over the meat to cook it slightly. Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy!
